Apps

Personal health & fitness tools

A small collection of free, independently-built web apps for tracking your own fitness — body measurements, calorie and macro targets, and more. Each app runs entirely in your browser; if you choose to sign in with Google, your data is saved directly to your own Google Drive, never to a server we control. See our Privacy Policy for details.

MacroMate

Calculate your daily calories and personalized protein, carbohydrate, and fat targets.

Caliper

Measure and track your chest, waist, arms, thighs, calves, and other body measurements.

Ironclad

Log your workouts day by day — exercises, sets, reps, weight, and how hard each set felt.
