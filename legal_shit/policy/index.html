Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

This policy covers the apps hosted on this site (including Caliper and MacroMate). These are personal, independently-built tools — not a company or commercial service. This page explains what data each app touches, where it's stored, and what is never done with it.

What data we access

Some apps on this site offer an optional "Sign in with Google" feature so your data can follow you across devices. If you use it, the app requests:

  • Your basic Google account info (name and email address), to show you're signed in.
  • Access to Google Sheets and Google Drive, but only for files the app itself creates on your behalf (for example, a spreadsheet named "Caliper Measurements"). The app cannot see, open, or modify any other file already in your Drive.

If you never click "Sign in with Google," none of this applies — the app simply saves your data in your browser's local storage instead (see below).

Where your data actually lives

We do not operate a server or database that stores your data. Depending on whether you sign in:

  • Signed out: your entries are saved only in your own browser's local storage, on your own device.
  • Signed in with Google: your entries are written directly into a spreadsheet created in your own Google Drive. It belongs to you, not to us — we never see, read, or download it. You can open, edit, export, or delete it at any time like any other file in your Drive.

What we don't do

  • We don't run ads, and we don't sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party.
  • We don't use analytics or tracking scripts to monitor your activity.
  • We don't store copies of your Sheets, your account info, or your measurements anywhere on our end.
  • We don't use your data to train any AI/ML models.

Revoking access

You can disconnect an app from your Google account at any time from your Google Account permissions page. Signing out inside the app itself also removes its access on that device. Revoking access does not delete the spreadsheet already created in your Drive — that stays under your control, and you're free to delete it yourself whenever you like.

Children's privacy

These apps are not directed at children and are not knowingly used to collect information from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change too. Continued use of the apps after an update means you accept the revised policy.
